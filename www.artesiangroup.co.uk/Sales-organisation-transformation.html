Transforming meetings programme (1 day)

Everyone wants the best possible results from meetings. This programme assumes that the quality of a group’s decisions and actions depends on the thinking they do first.

Who is it for?

Leaders who want to increase the effectiveness of meetings.

What is included?

During the programme we will cover Thinking Pairs TM, Dialogue, Rounds, Open discussions, The Time To Think CouncilTM, Presentations and putting it together in a ‘transformed meeting’.

Breakthrough Coaching Course (5 days)

Who is it for?

Business leaders coaching others as part of a line role. HR leaders. Coaches. Facilitators. Consultants.

This course will increase your ability to help clients achieve breakthroughs in their thinking. This is desperately needed in our world. Most coaches help clients achieve goals, assess where they are, explore options and commit to action. Many coaches also regularly give advice and input, whether or not the client explicitly requested it.
