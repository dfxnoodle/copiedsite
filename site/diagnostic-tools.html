Artesian

What business does a medieval ‘walking meditation’ have to do with the way we work today? Speaking from our rich experience, we believe it will unlock the potential in your people, speak to their heart, release their power and help them to assess who they are and where they’re going, individually and collectively – through a multi-sensory journey that combines ancient reflective practice with contemporary interactive, business-focused exercises.

In fact, labyrinth offers an extraordinary chance to reflect in a unique and dynamic way on the particular issues affecting you, as you seek to capitalise financially, corporately, socially, emotionally and spiritually in today’s ever-changing world.

It’s an indoor, circular walk with a difference – a pathway with a beginning, middle and end, that generates inspiration, instead of force-feeding you information. It’s an experience that calls forth wisdom instead of simply storing-up knowledge.

Labyrinth is both arresting and artistic yet specifically tailored to suit your own business requirements. We will co-create a path that takes you into the areas you specifically wish to explore together – such as leadership, vision, vocation, identity, teamship, gifts and strengths, emotional intelligence, relationships, story-telling, wholeness, growth, self-awareness, creativity, and so much more…
