Artesian

We select our Associates using three main criteria:

  • Alignment with our values
  • Skills and experience - most Associates are former leaders in commerce and industry, qualified psychologists or experienced leadership consultants, and we also use professional actors and musicians.
  • Approach and fit with our methodology - we always work with Associates initially to make sure they deliver the Artesian approach to our clients. Our on boarding process now includes a facilitator development programme, offered to new Associates.

We can offer facilitators and coaches in most regions of the world, who work in many different languages.

We understand that our work involves building high trust, credible relationships very quickly, and that matching our associates with our clients is a key part of our success. We involve our clients in this process, and work with them to understand who will be the best fit for their organisation and their needs.

Selected Associates

We present a selection of our associates, below. Contact us to find out more.

Clive Barrow

(Hong Kong and New Zealand)

Clive has 20 years’ experience as a coach and facilitator. He was Managing Director of the Mountain Valley Development Centre in Hawkes Bay between 2005 and 2010. Clive has also led successful expeditions over past 20 years to Chile, Guyana, Kenya, Tanzania, and the Himalayas, including ascent of over 10 summits of over 10,000ft. He has also been a Choral Scholar in Winchester Cathedral, a Regular Army Officer and is a Consultant to Royal Geographical Society on expedition risk assessment and crisis management (annual seminars, published in Expedition Medicine Handbook).

Daphne Clifton (UK)

Daphne is an Executive Coach and trainer with over 25 years experience in business management, sales and customer service. Daphne has spent 20 years in Outdoor Advertising as both a media buyer and seller. She is a member of the International Coach Federation, the C.I.P.D. and is a Director of the South East London Chamber of Commerce. The author of ‘Network with Confidence’ and ‘Franchising on a Shoestring’ Daphne is also a speaker, facilitator and radio commentator.

Jeremy Cox (UK)

For the last 16 years, Jeremy has worked with business leaders throughout the world to help prepare them for complex and challenging times. He works in four main areas; Conference Speaking, Facilitating Board and Team Offsites, Designing and Delivering Leadership Workshops, and Coaching Business Leaders in defining and achieving High Performance. Previously Jeremy had a varied career as a musician and as an Officer in the British Army.

Shelley Gaynes (USA)

Shelley has extensive corporate experience in sales, management, training, and coaching. She is known for her excellent communications skills, high energy, and ability to improve performance. She is highly skilled at executive coaching, and corporate course facilitation, and holds an Executive MBA and BA in Psychology.  Shelley has built a B2B sales organization from the ground-up. As a Regional Sales Manager for Egghead Software, she established and led the fastest growing corporate division with revenue responsibility of over $25 Million

David Hasenbalg (USA)

Dave Hasenbalg has spent the last 20 years leading, coaching, and consulting businesses to find solutions to real-world challenges by illuminating blind spots, building better teams, and coordinating action that produces results. He has an expertise in developing leaders, bringing about change in organizations of all sizes, and eliminating the impediments to effective execution. His practical background is as a military officer as well as a leader in cutting edge, non-profit, and Fortune 100 companies.

Israel Oyelumade (UK)

Israel graduated from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts (RADA), the leading conservatoire in the realm of dramatic arts. He was one of 28 successful candidates from among 15,000 applicants to join RADA. As a business coach and facilitator for the last seven years, Israel has worked with some of the world’s senior business professionals and leading communicators.

Sarah Pearce (UK)

Sarah is an Executive Coach, specialising in helping business leaders build strong, influential relationships for greater success. She works with the directors of financial services firms and blue chip companies, combining psychological insights with practical techniques towards lasting personal change. Sarah has a MA from Oxford University and is studying for a MA in Coaching & Mentoring at Oxford Brookes University. She is a Member of the Association for Coaching and a NLP Practitioner. Previously, Sarah spent 10 years working in direct sales to the financial services sector. 

Alper Tenguez (Turkey & Germany)

Alper is a versatile and sought-after executive coach. Clients value his professional credibility in business and his custom-made personal development journeys for individuals and teams. He draws on international work experience in Europe, Middle East, Asia and North America where he coaches and teaches in corporate development programs in pharma, industry, and professional services.

Alper pairs this practice with his position as a lecturer and post-doctoral researcher in management development at the Goethe University in Frankfurt, Germany. In his first career Alper worked as an Engagement Manager with McKinsey in particular on large-scale and long-term transformational programs. Alper works in German, Turkish and English and can communicate informally in Spanish, French and Japanese.
